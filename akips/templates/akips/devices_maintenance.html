AKiPS uses "maintenance mode" to disable polling for a device. While in maintenance, the device state will not change. If the device went down then was put into maintenance mode, the status will stay "down" until it leaves maintenance, polling resumes, and the "up" state is measured. Human interaction is needed to move devices in/out of AKiPS maintenance mode.
